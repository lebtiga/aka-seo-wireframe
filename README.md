# ⚡ AKA SEO Wireframe

> A 3-tier topical content framework for building authority sites that rank on both Google and AI-powered search (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, AI Overviews).

A free framework by [Rob Rizk](https://github.com/lebtiga). Works with any LLM — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, or your AI tool of choice.

## The Framework

**A**uthority → **K**nowledge → **A**nswer

Three tiers that mirror how both Google AND LLMs build their understanding of a topic:

```
Authority hub (5-7 per site) — 4,000 words, head keyword, broad coverage
    ↓
Knowledge pages (12-15 per hub) — 2,000 words, mid-tail, deep-dive topics
    ↓
Answer pages (20-30 per hub) — 1,000 words, long-tail Q&A, snippet-optimized
```

**Total per site:** ~205 pages with 1,500+ internal links and proper topical clustering.

## Why It Works

### 🤖 LLMs reward this structure

LLMs build internal knowledge graphs from your site structure. AKA gives them:
- Authority signals at the top
- Depth signals in the middle
- Direct answer formats at the bottom

Result: Your content gets cited in AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity — instead of skipped.

### 🔍 Google rewards this structure

The 2024 Helpful Content Updates demoted "wide but shallow" content. AKA forces:
- **Topical authority** (5-7 hubs covering an entire niche)
- **EEAT signals** (Authority hubs establish trust, Knowledge demonstrates expertise)
- **Helpful content** (comprehensive + deep + connected)

## How to Use This With Your AI

### Option 1 — Paste into a system prompt

Copy the contents of `SKILL.md` and paste it as a system prompt or "custom instructions" in your AI tool:

- **ChatGPT:** Settings → Custom Instructions, OR start a new chat with the SKILL.md content as the first message
- **Claude:** Start a new conversation with SKILL.md as the first message, OR add it to a Project's knowledge base
- **Gemini:** Paste into Custom Gem instructions
- **Perplexity:** Paste at the start of a Pro Search session

Then ask: *"Plan an AKA-structured authority site for [your niche] in [your location]."*

### Option 2 — Use as a reference document

Save `SKILL.md` to your project. Reference it whenever you brief any AI tool on building authority site content. Works as a methodology guide for human content teams too.
